YourMD.Online is a subsidiary of United Medical Group (UMG) was founded in 2019. UMG has now designed and built a telemedicine electronic health record (EHR) system that is current, and more efficient than existing EHR in the marketplace . YourMD.Online was created by a doctor for the purpose of improving patient-doctor telemedicine encounters.

Patients can now receive much needed guidance, triage care for their pressing healthcare questions and basic needs when they need it the most. We are deploying an affordable care platform to mitigate ever growing healthcare costs. This is our leap toward a healthcare system that can be inclusive and provide a safe solution for communities deprived of medical professionals.

YourMD.Online stands apart from other telemedicine solutions in its approach by utilizing our growing network of United Medical Group providers from various specialties and also standardizing the cost of care with a catch all pricing model. We believe the future belongs to individuals who have full control over their health and the providers who prioritize their patients over bureaucracy. We echo United Medical Group's approach, that is, to simply focus on patient satisfaction and outcomes.
